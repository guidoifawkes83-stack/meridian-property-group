Property Overview

An Architectural Masterpiece with Panoramic Vistas

Perched above the harbour with commanding panoramic views from every principal room, this extraordinary residence represents the pinnacle of North Shore luxury living. Designed by award-winning architect Marcus Reid and completed in 2019, the home masterfully integrates indoor and outdoor living across two expansive levels.

The ground floor unfolds as a series of connected living spaces — a formal lounge, bespoke chef's kitchen with butler's pantry, and an open dining room that flows seamlessly to a north-facing terrace. Retractable glass walls dissolve the boundary between interior and exterior, creating an entertainer's paradise against the backdrop of the harbour.

The upper level is dedicated to rest and retreat. The master suite is a sanctuary unto itself — a generous bedroom with harbour views, a fully tiled ensuite with freestanding bath, and a custom walk-in robe. Four additional bedrooms, each with built-in storage, are served by two further bathrooms of equal quality.

Set on 620sqm of north-east facing land in one of Mosman's most tightly-held streets, this is a rare opportunity to acquire a home of true architectural significance in Sydney's most coveted harbour suburb.
